FAQ

Common questions

How does the Free tier work?

Register for free, pick one SEA market, and receive weekly AI-generated market signals and summaries — no credit card required. Upgrade at any time to unlock leads, daily scans, and all 6 markets.

What data sources do you use?

Our autonomous pipeline scans PropertyGuru, 99.co, Rumah123, DDProperty, Lamudi, Batdongsan and many more across 6 SEA markets daily, processing signals through our AI classification engine.

What are the Special Services add-ons?

Special Services are advanced web data toolkits available to Growth, Pro, and Enterprise subscribers. They include capabilities like Web Unlocker API, Browser API, and Proxy Networks for deeper custom intelligence workflows.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All paid plans are month-to-month with no lock-in. Contact us at benny@seapropertypulse.com and we'll handle your cancellation within 24 hours.

What is the SGD 79 introductory offer?

New Agent plan subscribers pay only SGD 79 for their first month — giving you 30 days to fully assess the platform, leads quality, and market signals. From month 2, the plan automatically renews at SGD 99/mo. You can cancel anytime before renewal.

What currencies do you accept?

Agent and Agency plans are priced in SGD. The Developer plan is priced in USD. We accept major payment methods via Stripe and Billplz (FPX for Malaysian users). USD invoicing available for Developer and custom plans.
